# Trader Behavior Insights

## Objective
Analyze how Bitcoin market sentiment (Fear vs Greed) affects trader performance and behavior using Hyperliquid trading data.

## Datasets
- Historical trader data (Hyperliquid)
- Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index

## Tools
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

## Key Findings
- Traders take larger positions during Greed periods.
- Increased risk does not always lead to higher profits.
- Fear periods show more disciplined trading behavior.
- Market sentiment strongly influences buy/sell decisions.

## Conclusion
Market sentiment plays a critical role in trader decision-making and risk exposure. Understanding sentiment-driven behavior can help improve trading strategies and risk management.
